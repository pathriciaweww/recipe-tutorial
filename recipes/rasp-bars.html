Raspberry Bars

Description

The recipes have been selected because they present low-carb, high-fat options that anyone can use. If you are tracking your macros carefully, it would be helpful to create meal plans using these and other recipes so that you can monitor your daily intake. You'll find plenty of tools online to help support you in this too.

Ingredients

  • 30g almond meal
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 3 cups fresh or frozen raspberries
  • 450g cream cheese, room temperature
  • 120ml sour cream
  • 100ml double cream
  • 2 tsp liquid stevia, divided
  • 125ml coconut oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup + 50g coconut flour, reserve 2 tbsps for dusting

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180C and grease and lina a 15 x 20cm slice tin with baking paper.
  2. To make the crust, combine almond meal, coconut oil, salt and liquid stevia. Mix through 1 cup of coconut flour. Press into the bottom of the slice tin and bake for 12 minutes until slightly browned on top. Remove from the oven and let cool while making the topping.
  3. Place the lemon zest, rapberries, cream cheese, sour cream, double cream, 2 teaspoonsof liquid stevia and 1/3 cup of coconut flour in a mixer and mix until thoroughly combined. Spoon in an even layer over the base and chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
  4. Dust with the reserved coconut flour and cut into serving squares
